About Us


Theta, Maths and Programming Support

Usually the Theta, Maths and Programming Support is free, open to all students at the university, and we offer the following services:

  • Drop-in sessions (Term Time) - 1 hour slots for quick guidance on progamming, mathematics or statistics;
  • A quiet place to study;
  • Textbooks and IT resources;
  • Online resources - for when you can't use the Centre;
  • Work sheets to take away and core textbooks for reference, covering every aspect of mathematics;
  • A team of friendly tutors who are skilled teachers and include experts in most areas where maths is used;

House Rules

We can:

  • Help with mathematics and statistics topics generally;
  • Advise before you start a major project;
  • Advise how to approach tricky numerical problems;
  • Point you to useful resources;
  • Support you with extra sessions if need be;

We do not:

  • Help answer specific assessed questions;
  • Allow you to book multiple appointments. Book only one. If you need more help, the tutor will advise you on this.;
