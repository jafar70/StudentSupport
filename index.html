We Support You

Many students find that there is a maths element to their degree and can struggle with aspects of statistics, quantitative research or financial analysis. Don't worry, help is on hand. You will find that in maths, often each new subject builds on the one before and so it's important to keep up. Don't assume that things will become clearer later. If you are struggling, make sure that you deal with the problem immediately.

The EC Futures Career Fair

In March we hosted our annual Engineering & Computing Career fair and what another fantastic event it was!
We had 50 companies attend and a range of E&C students from all years who made the most out of the opportunity to speak to potential employers and leave a fantastic first impression.
The E&C career fair is an annual event and a perfect opportunity to find out more about the industry and build up your contacts. Our students dressed to impressed and came armed with CVs and plenty of questions!
